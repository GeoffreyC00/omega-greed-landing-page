About

Meet OmegaGreed

I'm Geoffrey Crawford, the creator behind OmegaGreed and Villains of Horror.

OmegaGreed is built for riders and enthusiasts who want more than spec sheets — real road trips, honest ownership stories, gear that actually gets used, and cinematic adventure from Los Angeles and beyond.

Through long-form YouTube, social content, and UGC, OmegaGreed helps brands reach engaged audiences who trust authentic recommendations and real-world testing.

Villains of Horror extends that same authentic approach into haunted attractions, horror events, conventions, and entertainment culture.

Behind the camera, I bring 7+ years of professional digital marketing experience — so partnerships are grounded in audience strategy, strong creative, and content built to perform.
